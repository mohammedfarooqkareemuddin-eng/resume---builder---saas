Privacy Policy – ResumeRocket

Last updated: June 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to ResumeRocket (resumerocket.tech). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our free resume building service.

2. Information We Collect

We DO NOT collect or store any personal information.

ResumeRocket is designed with your privacy in mind. All resume creation happens directly in your browser. Your data is processed locally on your device and is not sent to our servers.

We do NOT use cookies for tracking. We only use essential cookies to keep your session active, and these do not store any personal data.

3. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal data, we do not use your information for any purpose. Your resume data stays on your device and is never shared with anyone.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our site. Google Analytics collects anonymous data such as page views, time spent, and general location (country level). It does not collect personal information like your name, email, or resume content.

You can learn more about Google's privacy practices here: Google Privacy Policy

5. Your Rights

You have the right to know what data we collect about you. Since we collect NO personal data, there is no data to access, correct, or delete.

6. Children's Privacy

ResumeRocket does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Our service is intended for job seekers of all ages, but we do not target children specifically.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

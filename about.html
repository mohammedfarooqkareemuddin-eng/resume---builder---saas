About ResumeRocket

From zero coding to building a free resume builder for the world – in just 7 months.

Our Story

ResumeRocket was born from a simple frustration: why do job seekers have to pay so much for a professional resume?

I'm a solo developer from Hyderabad, India. 7 months ago, I had zero coding experience. I used free AI tools, determination, and countless late nights to learn, build, and launch ResumeRocket.

Today, ResumeRocket helps thousands of job seekers across the US, UK, India, and other countries build professional resumes that get past HR filters – completely free.

Our Mission

To provide 100% free, professional resume tools to every job seeker on the planet. No sign-ups. No hidden charges. No data collection. Just a tool that works.

Why ResumeRocket?

  • 100% Free – No credit card, no subscription, no trial periods.
  • No Sign-Up – Just open and start building your resume instantly.
  • ATS-Friendly – Templates designed to pass automated HR filters.
  • Privacy First – Your data never leaves your device.
  • Made in India – Built with ❤️ from Hyderabad.

Our Vision

We believe that professional tools should not be a privilege. Everyone deserves access to quality career resources, regardless of their budget. We're building a future where free, professional tools are the norm, not the exception.
